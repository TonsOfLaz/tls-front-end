SB1441/HB2149  

Expands eligibility for the Fallen Heroes Medal.

Category VETERANS & MILITARY AFFAIRS
Summary Expands eligibility for a Fallen Heroes Medal to service members killed in attacks specifically targeting service members or attacks that are carried out by an individual who has been inspired or organized by a foreign terrorist organization. Expands eligibility to all service members stationed in the state or who are legal residents of the state.
