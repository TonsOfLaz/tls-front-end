SB127
HB24
Go Build Tennessee Act. Establishes the Go Build Tennessee Program, to be administered by a designated corporation, for the purpose of securing funding to promote and foster the development of a statewide program designed to increase career opportunities for secondary and post-secondary students in the construction industry. Provides that the program shall be funded by a Go Build Account within the board of licensing contractors, and the corporation shall have access to the funding for the purpose of performing its duties set forth in this act. ( S: Sen. Tracy, Jim  H: Rep. Williams, Ryan )
04/22/15 - Senate passed with amendment 6.
04/22/15 - House concurred in Senate amendment 6.
05/27/15 - Enacted as Public Chapter 0500 effective May 20, 2015.

SB1283
HB1214
Inspection of a footer to be considered performed timely. Increases the number of days for an inspection of a footer to be considered performed timely from inspection within one working day of when the request is made to the inspector, to three working days. ( S: Sen. Hensley, Joey  H: Rep. Doss, Barry )
03/31/15 - Failed in Senate Commerce & Labor Committee for lack of a second.
04/01/15 - Taken off notice in House Business & Utilities Committee.
