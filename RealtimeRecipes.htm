Buffalo chicken Brooklyn pizza
a delicious pizza recipe, dough is made from scratch, this is not a quick and easy recipe!
Begin

Ingredients

bread flour 2 cups n/a
sea salt 1 teaspoon n/a
dry active yeast. .75 teaspoon n/a
Olive oil 1 teaspoon n/a
pizza sauce 2 tablespoons n/a
buffalo sauce 2 tablespoons n/a
mozzarella cheese 3/4 cup n/a
gorgonzola cheese 1/4 cup crumbled n/a
cornmeal 1/2 tablespoon n/a

Instructions

  • STEP 1

    add 1 cup of lukewarm water to bowl, and add in the yeast and oil. Let sit for 3 to 5 minutes

  • STEP 2

    mix the flour and salt together in a mixing bowl, and combine by mixing with a wooden spoon. Keep on adding flour 1/8 cup at a time, until the dough stops sticking to the sides. then, start to knead with your flour coated hands for 3 minutes. Start the timer before you get your hands dirty!

  • 15:00

    STEP 3

    let dough rest for 15 minutes

  • STEP 4

    knead again for 3 minutes. I had to add more flour again, because it stuck to the bowl.

  • STEP 5

    cut dough into 2 pieces, coat in Olive oil, and add each one to its own bowl. cover bowl, and place in fridge for 4 to 8 hours to rise.

  • 30:00

    STEP 6

    let the dough rest for 30 minutes, out of the fridge.

  • STEP 7

    preheat oven, and pizzastone if you have it, to 550.

  • STEP 8

    add cornmeal and flour to a wooden Curtin board. add the dough, stretching out carefully in a circular motion, until dough is thin, but not risking ripping.

  • 06:00

    STEP 9

    add 1 cup of lukewarm water to bowl, and add in the yeast and oil. Let sit for 3 to 5 minutes

  • STEP 10

    add two tablespoons of pizza sauce, and 1 tablespoon of buffalo sauce to the dough.

  • STEP 11

    mix the mozzarella and gorgonzola cheese and spread over the sauce.

  • STEP 12

    top with chicken pieces, and sprinkle the remaining tablespoon of buffalo sauce over the chicken. You can add more if you like, depending on the heat of the sauce, tolerance, etc.

  • STEP 13

    make sure the dough can be rocked off the cutting board, and Rock it onto the pizza stone, in one swift motion.

  • STEP 14

    cook for 10 to 12 minutes.

  • STEP 15

    let cool for 5 minutes, after removing from oven. then slice it into 8 or 6 pieces.
